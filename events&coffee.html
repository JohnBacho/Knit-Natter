Events & Coffee – Where Knitting and Connection Meet

At Knit & Natter, we know knitting is more than a hobby-it’s a way to bring people together. We host an array of events, coffee meetups, and workshops designed to inspire creativity, foster friendships, and build a strong, welcoming community. Be it in person or virtually, there’s room for you.

On Going Events

Knit & Sip – Weekly Coffee Meetup

Every Saturday | 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM

Various Cafés & Virtual Sessions

Grab your favorite drink and your latest project and join us for a morning of knitting and conversation. Whether you’re a seasoned knitter or just starting, our weekly Knit & Sip meetups are the perfect place to share tips and enjoy good company.

Maker’s Spotlight- Show & Tell Night

2nd Thursday of Every Month | 8:00 PM (EST)

Virtual Event

Share your latest knitting creations, get feedback, and rejoice in the ingenuity of our community! Be it a spectacular sweater or finally mastering that intricate technique, now is the perfect time to show off your creativity among fellow knitters.

Knitting 101 – Beginner’s Workshop

Every Wednesday | 7:00 PM (EST)

Various Cafés & Virtual Sessions

New to knitting? Our Beginner’s Workshop is just for you! Learn the basics, from casting on to creating your first project. This interactive class will have you knitting with confidence in no time. Join us and start your knitting journey today!

Pattern of the Month Club

New Pattern Released Every 1st of the Month

Available Online for Members

Want new ideas? Each month we publish a different, EXCLUSIVE knitting pattern that members try. Your needles will always enjoy something exciting, from basic projects for total beginners to designs with advanced stitch patterns. Plus, you'll get access to a supportive community where you can share your progress and get tips from fellow knitters.

Previous Events

Cozy Winter Knit Fest – A Celebration of Stitches

From novices to serious knitting enthusiasts, Cozy Winter Knit Fest had been warmly celebrated as a festival of creativity and community. And the day filled with live demonstrations of knitting techniques, workshops taken by the experienced, and above all, fun speed-knit challenges.

One of many highlights was a “Secret Stitch Swap” where participants exchanged their mystery knitting patterns to make surprise gifts for the others. This was followed up with a wrap-up fireside chat replete with hot cocoa, much laughter, and loads of yarn inspiration.

Spring Stitch Retreat – A Weekend of Creativity

Our Spring Stitch Retreat was a refreshing weekend getaway that was sure to inspire and rejuvenate the beginning and experienced knitter alike. Nestled in a cozy lakeside lodge, attendees enjoyed a mix of hands-on knitting workshops, mindfulness sessions in knitting, and nature walks to spark creativity.

Additional weekend activities included guests from the fiber arts industry, a make-your-own-yarn station, and a knitting circle by the fireplace. But of course, the most special thing was our “Mystery Knit Challenge,” whereby participants received surprise patterns to complete by the end of the retreat.
